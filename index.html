Cadence Philosophy
Bi-weekly email. Bi-weekly text. Alternating. Never spammy.
Week 1: Email. Week 2: Text from Sally. Week 3: Email. Week 4: Text. That's the rhythm. Four touchpoints a month — two emails, two texts — enough to stay top of mind without wearing out the welcome. As engagement grows, we optimize based on open and response data.
The Sally Text Formula
"Hi [First Name], it's Sally — Matthew's assistant at Intellabooks. Just checking in! If you need anything from Matthew, he's always a call away. Hope your week is going well. 😊" Simple, warm, no ask. Relationship first.
